# Media verification — images & video (high-level checklist)

## Goal
Avoid “context collapse”: real media reused with false date/location/caption.

## Practical checks
- **Context**: what event is claimed (who/what/when/where)? Is the caption specific or vague?
- **Reverse image search** (when available) to find earliest appearances and original context.
- **Look for cropping / edits**: mismatched shadows, inconsistent fonts, unnatural edges, repeated patterns.
- **Check independent coverage**: major events usually have multiple independent reports.
- **Beware “urgent share” framing**: emotional calls to action increase misinformation spread.

## When to label “unknown”
If the media is plausible but you cannot establish the original context, treat the claim as **unverified**.
